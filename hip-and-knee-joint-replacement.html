Hip & Knee Joint Replacement at Chirayu Clinic

By Dr. Anish Jain – March 2026

Joint pain from arthritis, injury, or wear-and-tear can significantly affect your quality of life. At Chirayu Clinic, Dr. Anish Jain offers advanced joint replacement procedures backed by over 25 years of orthopedic surgical experience.

Procedures We Offer

  • TKR (Total Knee Replacement) – for severe knee arthritis affecting the entire joint
  • UKA (Unicompartmental Knee Arthroplasty) – partial knee replacement when damage is limited to one compartment
  • THR (Total Hip Replacement) – for hip joints damaged by arthritis or fracture

Our approach focuses on reliable treatment, accurate diagnosis, and excellent surgical outcomes. Digital X-ray facilities at the clinic allow precise pre-operative planning.

Ayushman Bharat Coverage

Eligible patients can avail joint replacement surgery under the Ayushman Bharat (Ayushman Yojana) scheme at Chirayu Clinic, making quality orthopedic care more accessible.

If you or a loved one is experiencing persistent joint pain, stiffness, or difficulty walking, consult Dr. Anish Jain at Chirayu Clinic. Early evaluation can help determine whether joint replacement is the right option for you.
