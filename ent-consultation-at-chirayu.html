ENT Consultation & Endoscopy at Chirayu Clinic

By Dr. Rashmi Jain – February 2026

Ear, nose, and throat problems are among the most common health complaints — from chronic sinusitis and hearing loss to tonsil infections and throat disorders. At Chirayu Clinic, Dr. Rashmi Jain provides comprehensive ENT care with over 25 years of experience.

What is ENT Endoscopy?

ENT endoscopy uses a thin, flexible scope with a camera to examine the ear canal, nasal passages, and throat. This allows Dr. Rashmi Jain to diagnose conditions accurately without invasive procedures, and to plan the most effective treatment.

Conditions We Treat

  • Chronic sinusitis and nasal blockages (FESS surgery when needed)
  • Ear infections, hearing problems, and tympanoplasty
  • Tonsillitis requiring tonsillectomy
  • Throat infections and voice disorders
  • Allergies affecting the upper respiratory tract

Having ENT endoscopy available on-site at Chirayu Clinic means you can receive diagnosis and specialist consultation in one visit — saving time and ensuring continuity of care alongside our orthopedic services.
