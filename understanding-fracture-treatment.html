Expert Fracture Care: Humerus Surgery Case Study

By Dr. Anish Jain – February 2026

Bone fractures require prompt and skilled treatment to ensure proper healing and restore function. At Chirayu Clinic, Dr. Anish Jain specializes in fracture management — from simple breaks to complex cases requiring surgical intervention.

Case Highlight: Humerus Fracture in a 74-Year-Old Patient

A recent case involved a 74-year-old patient with a severe humerus (upper arm bone) fracture. Pre-operative X-rays revealed a complex break that required surgical fixation. Using an intramedullary nail with stabilizing screws, the fracture was successfully repaired.

Post-operative X-rays confirmed excellent alignment and fixation, giving the patient the best chance for a full recovery. This case demonstrates that age should not be a barrier to receiving quality fracture surgery when performed by an experienced orthopedic surgeon.

When to Seek Help

  • Severe pain after a fall or accident
  • Visible deformity or swelling in a limb
  • Inability to move or bear weight on the affected area
  • Cracking or grinding sensation at the injury site
