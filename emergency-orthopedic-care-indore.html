Accident & Emergency Orthopedic Care in Indore

By Dr. Anish Jain – January 2026

Accidents and sudden injuries can happen at any time. When they involve bones, joints, or soft tissue, getting prompt orthopedic care is critical to prevent complications and ensure the best recovery outcome.

One Call Away

At Chirayu Clinic, Dr. Anish Jain provides accident and emergency orthopedic care in Indore. Whether it's a fracture from a road accident, a sports injury, or a fall at home, our clinic is equipped to assess and treat orthopedic emergencies.

What to Do After an Injury

  1. Keep the injured area as still as possible
  2. Apply ice to reduce swelling (if there is no open wound)
  3. Do not try to realign a deformed limb yourself
  4. Call Chirayu Clinic immediately at 98260 80225
  5. Come to the clinic for digital X-ray and specialist evaluation

With on-site digital X-ray, ECG, and pharmacy facilities, Chirayu Clinic provides comprehensive emergency support so you don't need to visit multiple locations during a stressful time.
