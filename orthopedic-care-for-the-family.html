Orthopedic & ENT Care for the Whole Family

Chirayu Clinic Team – December 2025

Healthcare needs vary across every stage of life — from a child's ear infection to a grandparent's knee replacement. Chirayu Clinic is uniquely positioned to serve families in Indore with two specialist doctors under one roof.

Two Specialities, One Clinic

Dr. Anish Jain handles orthopedic concerns — fractures, joint pain, arthritis, sports injuries, and surgical procedures including joint replacement. Dr. Rashmi Jain manages all ENT conditions — sinus problems, ear infections, throat disorders, and endoscopic procedures.

This dual-speciality setup means your family can visit one trusted clinic for both bone & joint and ear, nose & throat care, with consistent follow-up and coordinated treatment.

Why Families Choose Chirayu

  • 25+ years of experience from both doctors
  • Digital X-ray, ENT endoscopy, ECG, and physiotherapy on-site
  • Morning and evening consulting hours for working families
  • Affordable consultation fees and insurance/Ayushman Bharat acceptance
  • Convenient location in Gumasta Nagar, behind Ranjeet Hanuman Temple

Your health is our priority. Visit Chirayu Clinic for compassionate, expert care for every member of your family.
